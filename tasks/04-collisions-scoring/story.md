# Story: Collisions And Scoring

As a player, I want clear rewards and fair deaths so that the score reflects survival and honey collection.

## Scope

- AABB collision
- smaller player hitbox
- honey collection
- spike death
- score calculation
- local best score

## Acceptance Criteria

- Collecting honey removes it and adds points.
- Hitting a spike ends the game.
- Score grows with time survived.
- Best score persists locally.
